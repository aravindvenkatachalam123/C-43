Wrestling

Character:
1.Player1
2.Robot

Goal:
1.Any one player or robot should kill another

Rules :
1.Each player and robot will be getting 2 powers
namely kick,punch
2.Player or Players can escape using left and right
arrow keys
3.Each will be given 5 lives
4.Who destroys the 5 lives of other wins the game
5.If the player does not lose 5 lives in round 1(2 minutes)
he will move to the next round
6.If the players loses in the round 2 he will start from the first
7.There are three rounds in this game
